About Attention Deficit Radio

This is a music hack created at London Music Hack Day by Paul Lamere. It is powered by The Echo Nest. This hack was created using the same technology as the Infinite Jukebox, Infinite Gangnam Style and Bohemian Rhapsichord.

The app creates a Pandora-like radio experience for those with very short attention spans. It will play around 30 seconds of a song and then will transition to the next track. The transitions are cross faded and beat matched to (hopefully) give you a seamless listening experience even across song transitions.
