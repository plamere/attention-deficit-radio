Swap the Drop

Take control of your favorite songs

Controls:

  • mouse click - start playing from the clicked beat
  • space - start and stop playing the current driver (space for all drivers)
  • arrows - control song cursor velocity for the current driver (shift/arrow for all drivers)
  • w/a/s/d - move the song cursor for the current driver (shift w/a/s/d for all drivers)
  • < > ? - controls the master beat period
  • b - toggle between beats and tatums
  • shift+number - bookmark a beat
  • command - cycle the though drivers
Things you can do:
  • Cross fade between two parts of a song.
  • Play two songs beat-aligned.
  • Try playing every other beat of a song by starting a song playing and then pressing the right arrow once.
  • Try playing a song backwards by pressing the left arrow twice and clicking on a beat.
  • Make a swing version of the song by switching to tatums (hit 'b') and setting the beat period to 150% (use the '>' key) and play every other tatum.
  • Create a 'breakdown' and a drop by stopping the song on a beat and reducing the period to zero (via the < key). start things back up by pressing '?' and up arrow simultaneously.
  • You can move blocks around by dragging them.
  • You can copy blocks with ctrl-c.
